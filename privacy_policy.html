Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. Overview

Vendor Credit Importer ("the Application") is an internal business tool designed to import vendor credits from CSV files into QuickBooks Online. This privacy policy describes how we handle data when you use this application.

2. Information We Access

The Application accesses the following information from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Vendor information (names and IDs)
  • Chart of accounts (account names and IDs)
  • Tax codes
  • Vendor credit data that you choose to import

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the accessed information solely to:

  • Validate vendor names and account names
  • Create vendor credits in your QuickBooks Online account
  • Display import progress and results to you

4. Data Storage

The Application does not store any of your QuickBooks data permanently. All data is processed in real-time during import and is not retained after the process completes. Your CSV files remain on your local computer only.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 authentication to securely connect to QuickBooks Online. Your credentials are never stored by the Application. All communication with QuickBooks uses encrypted HTTPS connections.

6. Third-Party Access

The Application does not share your data with any third parties. Data is only transmitted between your computer and QuickBooks Online's API.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke the Application's access to QuickBooks at any time through your QuickBooks account settings
  • Delete any vendor credits created by the Application directly in QuickBooks
  • Request information about how your data is used by contacting us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
